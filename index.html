Exotic South American Restaurant

At Cafe Durmiente, our mission is to provide our guests with the most authentic Ecuadorian experience outside of Ecuador.

To provide a one-of-a-kind experience that energizes everyone with an enthusiastic welcome, exceptional service, awesome food, killer tunes, and an unforgettable time. To create an environment where absolute guest satisfaction is our highest priority. Our vision is to have locations all over the world and be known as the brand for quality meals, a great crew, cool culture, and active community involvement. To serve the world flavor.
